¿Why WTS?

WTS is a US trading company with 25 years of leadership in Textile Industry, with offices in New York, Los Angeles, Peru and Guatemala. Working with more than 40 of the best brands in the United States; offering services ranging from designing to production planning and tracking.

We are made up of an exceptional team of experts in the field, and so committed to achieving the highest standards of service through competitive prices, quality of service and reliable delivery; offering assertive and efficient communication to our customers.

At WTS we offer a comprehensive and integral service, which includes design, product development, laboratory, testing services, cloth development, planning, production tracking, quality control and logistics.


SERVICES

Design

We have a team specialized in design and development that constantly creates innovative proposals for our clients. We offer a comprehensive and integral service covering trends research, textile design, creation of collections, mold making, prototyping and development of new finishing, washing and stamping techniques.

Development of fabrics

Our team specialized in textile development, together with the design area, permanently creates new proposals for fabrics, exploring new materials, blend of fibers, types of yarn, textile structure, finishes and special techniques. We also have the ability to duplicate specific samples sent by our customers, adapting them to their requirements in terms of costs, quality standards and appearance.

Product Development

Our Product Development area is made up of an experienced team that supervises and approves all stages of the pre-production process such as prototypes, fit samples, sales samples and pp samples, from the detailed analysis of the construction of the samples originals or the tech packs sent by our clients.

Customer Service

Our expert team in customer service is responsible for meeting the requirements and sales opportunities of customers as a vital part of the value chain. We coordinate the execution of the process with the different actors involved (customer, suppliers, internal support areas) in order to satisfy the need, offering alternatives, competitive prices in the times that the business requires.

Laboratory

We have a textile quality laboratory managed by a team of certified AATCC technicians, in which we evaluate colors, fabrics and garments at all stages of the production process. This allows us to have an efficient control over the quality of each of the dyeing lots that will be used in the production; using the AATCC and ASTM standards tests.

Planning and Monitoring

WTS is characterized by providing support to its clients in all its areas, specifically in the area of monitoring and control of production, our team evaluates each of the placements from the intention of the placement in order to offer and / or confirm the date of Office, in turn we provide support to our customers and factories so that the proposed leadtimes are feasible in the execution.

Quality Control System:

WTS has a system of assurance of quality control from the textile stage to finished product, we work with our suppliers in the implementation of quality assurance processes and in turn accompany and monitor the processes with the purpose of achieving garments that meet and exceed Customer expectations.

Logistics:

American company that is dedicated specifically to the handling of textile product. They have a distribution platform in the USA that allows us to give the delivery service to the customer in their warehouses. Wherever the customer's warehouses are located in the US we are able to dispatch the merchandise where requested by the customer.
