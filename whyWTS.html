The fastest supply model in the region

WTS is

A US trading company with 25 years of leadership in Textile Industry, with offices in New York, Los Angeles, Peru and Guatemala. Working with more than 40 of the best brands in the United States; offering services ranging from designing to production planning and tracking.

We are

Made up of an exceptional team of experts in the field, and so committed to achieving the highest standards of service through competitive prices, quality of service and reliable delivery; offering assertive and efficient communication to our customers.

At WTS

We offer a comprehensive and integral service, which includes design, product development, laboratory, testing services, cloth development, planning, production tracking, quality control and logistics.
