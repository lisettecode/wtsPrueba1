DESIGN

We have a team specialized in design and development that constantly creates innovative proposals for our clients. We offer a comprehensive and integral service covering trends research, textile design, creation of collections, mold making, prototyping and development of new finishing, washing and stamping techniques.
Additionally we work hand in hand with our clients to design specific requirements like stamping, special washes, creation of styles and complete collections.

FABRIC DEVELOPMENT

Our team specialized in textile development, together with the design area, permanently creates new proposals for fabrics, exploring new materials, blend of fibers, types of yarn, textile structure, finishes and special techniques.
We also have the ability to duplicate specific samples sent by our customers, adapting them to their requirements in terms of costs, quality standards and appearance. Using the great experience of our team, we also provide technical support to all our suppliers.

PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT

Our Product Development area is made up of an experienced team that supervises and approves all stages of the pre-production process such as prototypes, fit samples, sales samples and pp samples, from the detailed analysis of the construction of the samples originals or the tech packs sent by our clients, until the preparation of the technical sheets and creation of the molds. The experience gained through interaction with different types of customers, allows us to provide highly qualified support to all our suppliers.

CUSTOMER SERVICE

Our expert team in customer service is responsible for meeting the requirements and sales opportunities of customers as a vital part of the value chain. We coordinate the execution of the process with the different actors involved (customer, suppliers, internal support areas) in order to satisfy the need, offering alternatives, competitive prices in the times that the business requires.
Among the activities we perform are the reception and processing of production orders, sales samples, developments and the follow-up to delivery, executing each stage with transparency, responsibility and business vision.

LABORATORY

We have a textile quality laboratory managed by a team of certified AATCC technicians, in which we evaluate colors, fabrics and garments at all stages of the production process. This allows us to have an efficient control over the quality of each of the dyeing lots that will be used in the production. Using the AATCC and ASTM standards for our tests.

PLANNING AND MONITORING

WTS is characterized by providing support to its clients in all its areas, specifically in the area of monitoring and control of production, our team evaluates each of the placements from the intention of the placement in order to offer and / or confirm the date of Office, in turn we provide support to our customers and factories so that the proposed leadtimes are feasible in the execution.
At the stage of production processes a constant monitoring is given to ensure the fulfillment of the dates of dispatch, actively involving us in all processes and to be able to overcome possible problems that are present in production. We work together with our factories so that evolution in the productive processes and become competitive in the leadtimes of the productive processes

Quality Control System

WTS has a system of assurance of quality control from the textile stage to finished product, we work with our suppliers in the implementation of quality assurance processes and in turn accompany and monitor the processes with the purpose of achieving garments that meet and exceed Customer expectations.
Within this area we also have the social compliance assessment, which is done to all suppliers to ensure that they are within the social and industrial safety regulations in the facilities of each of the factories.
Physical resources composed of 15 professionals who constantly monitor the entire process.

LOGISTICS

American company that is dedicated specifically to the handling of textile product. They have a distribution platform in the USA that allows us to give the delivery service to the customer in their warehouses. Wherever the customer's warehouses are located in the US we are able to dispatch the merchandise where requested by the customer.
Kelly not only handles the issue of distribution, it also takes care of transporting the cargo from Peru to USA. This is what differentiates us from other companies, and we do it thanks to our strategic partner.
